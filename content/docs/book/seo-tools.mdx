### 1. [Google My Business](https://www.google.com/business/)

Google My Business is a free tool that helps businesses manage their online presence across Google, including Search and Maps. By verifying and editing your business information, you can help customers find you and tell them your story.

### 2. [Whitespark](https://whitespark.ca/)

Whitespark is a local SEO tool that specializes in local search and citation building. It offers a variety of services, including local search rank tracking, citation audits, and Google My Business optimization.

---

### 3. [Google Keyword Planner](https://ads.google.com/home/tools/keyword-planner/)

Google Keyword Planner is a free tool that helps you build Search Network campaigns by finding keyword ideas and estimating how they may perform. It can also help you choose competitive bids and budgets to use with your campaigns.

---

### 4. [Google Analytics and Google Search Console](https://analytics.google.com/)

Google Analytics and Google Search Console are free tools that help you track and analyze your website's performance. They provide valuable insights into your site's traffic, user behavior, and search engine visibility.
